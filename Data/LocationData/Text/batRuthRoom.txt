As you continue on your journey to find the love of your life, the smell of Cracker Jacks and Hot Dogs lures you down a narrow hallway lined with pictures of famous baseball players. As you open the door, you notice a traditional “man cave” lined with neon-lighted signs, a mini fridge full of cold drinks, and sports memorabilia covering the walls. And as you look to the couch situated in front of the TV, you are surprised to be greeted by the famous Bat Ruth!
