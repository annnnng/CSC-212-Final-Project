WELCOME TO THE HELP MENU
Here's the name of the game:
Enter "take" followed directly by your item to put it in your inventory.
Enter "drop" followed directly by your item to free it in your inventory.
Enter "north" "south" "east" or "west" in order to traverse through the rooms.
Enter "quit" if you'd like to end the game at any place. Note that if you quit, you must rerun the program in order to start a new game. 
Enter "return to living room" to return to the living room from any position in the game.
